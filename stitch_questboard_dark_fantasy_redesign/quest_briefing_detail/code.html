menu_book Quest Briefing

The tavern keeper of the 'Leaky Cauldron' has reported strange, ethereal disturbances in the lower cellars. Several kegs of premium ale have been inexplicably drained, and the temperature drops rapidly as one descends the stairs.

Local scholars suspect a minor tear in the veil, allowing restless spirits to manifest. Your task is to descend into the cellars, identify the source of the spectral incursion, and banish the entities before they can spread to the main hall. Proceed with caution; physical weapons may be less effective against ethereal foes.
